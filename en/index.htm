ABOUT
US

KTA is a community of people, who love Kyiv. We believe that by combining our own experience together with the most successful practices of other European cities we can successfully develop the travel industry of Kyiv, cherishing positive memories of our guests. Modern innovations, resources and support of the most active ambassadors of city discover opportunities for the cultural and economic benefits of the capital city.

Activity

Support

CONSULTING OF THE TOURIST INDUSTRY TO PROTECT ITS INTERESTS. REPRESENTATION ASSOCIATION MEMBERS IN STATE AUTHORITIES, LOCAL GOVERNMENTS AND INTERNATIONAL ORGANIZATIONS. PROMOTING PARTICIPANTS IN SOLVING THE PROBLEMS RELATING TO THEIR ACTIVITIES, AS PARTICIPANTS IN THE MARKET OF TOURISM SERVICES.

Promotion

SUPPORT AND DEVELOPMENT OF TOURISM IN THE CITY OF KYIV AND UKRAINE FOR SUCCESSFUL PROMOTION AND POPULARIZATION OF THE BRAND OF THE CITY IN UKRAINE AND THE OUTSIDE OF UKRAINE. PROMOTION OF THE CAPITAL AS DESTINATION TO PROMOTE INCREASE OF THE INTERNAL AND OUTDOOR FLOW OF THE TOURISTS.

Education

CREATION OF NEW PROJECTS FOR EDUCATIONAL DEVELOPMENT OF THE TOURIST DEPARTMENT OF KYIV AND UKRAINE. ASSISTANCE TO INCREASE QUALIFICATION AND PROFESSIONALISM OF ASSOCIATION PARTICIPANTS. SUPPORT FOR PROJECTS INTENDED TO IMPLEMENT THE CAPACITY OF THE CITY AND THE COUNTRY.

International Arena

PRESENTATION OF THE CAPITAL AND UKRAINE ON THE DESTINATION ON INTERNATIONAL EXHIBITIONS AND EVENTS. ACHIEVING NEW PARTNERS FORWARD FOR CREATION OF NEW PROJECTS AND COMPATIBLE CONTINGENCY EXISTING. ASSOCIATION OF PARTICIPANTS IN THE MARKET OF TOURISM SERVICES OF OTHER COUNTRIES, AWAY FOR EXCHANGE OF INFORMATION AND EXPERIENCE BETWEEN PARTICIPANTS.

Events

FOLLOW-UP PROJECTS AND SUPPORT FOR EXISTING BUSINESS AND EVENTS TOURISM. INVESTIGATION OF WORLD PROJECTS FOR THEIR ARRIVAL IN KYIV. PROMOTION OF THE INDUSTRIAL INVESTMENT INVESTMENT TO DEVELOP A TOURIST DEPARTMENT OF UKRAINE.

FREE CITY TAX

OFFICIAL FREE TOURIST COLLECTION "FREE CITY TAX" - COLLECTION OF FUNDS ON THE PROMOTION OF KYIV ON THE BASIS OF VOLUNTARY CONTRIBUTIONS TO TOURISTS ON THE EFFICIENCY OF KTA. THE ASSOCIATION IS PROCESSED WITHOUT MEMBERSHIP AND REALIZES OUR LEGAL, EDUCATIONAL AND PROMOTIONAL PROJECTS UNDER THE "FREE CITY TAX". "READ THE PROJECT"

Kyiv
Investment Forum 2017

A grand event was held in Kyiv – Kyiv Investment Forum, which a platform for the Kyiv government and business to discuss investment projects. In 2017 the forum focused on discussing the future of Kyiv as an international tourist destination and on investments in the urban tourism infrastructure. The panels of business tourism and film industry were fully organized by the Kyiv Tourist Association with the support from partners. The event was attended by more than 500 representatives from the business and investment community, central and local authorities and the media.

Kyiv
Tourism Awards 2017

Kyiv Tourism Awards is an annual award ceremony, which is intended to increase the standards of tourist services in the capital and to establish interaction among local tourist industry representatives for the development of Kyiv as a competitive European destination. Awards in 13 different categories include hotels, hostels, restaurants, best restaurants and tourist locations. In the year 2017 Kyiv's Mayor Vitali Klitschko was the honorable guest of the event. He noted that in recent years Kyiv's tourism has gained momentum and yields good results. Kyiv Tourist Association was a partner of this event and continues to increase the importance of service quality in Ukraine.
